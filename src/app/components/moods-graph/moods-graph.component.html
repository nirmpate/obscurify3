Your Moods

Happiness

Scores with a higher number sound more positive (e.g. happy, cheerful, euphoric), while tracks with a lower number sound more negative (e.g. sad, depressed, angry)

Danceability

Danceability describes how suitable your music is for dancing based on a combination of musical elements including tempo, rhythm stability, beat strength, and overall regularity.

Energy

Energy represents a perceptual measure of intensity and activity. Typically, energetic tracks feel fast, loud, and noisy.

Acousticness

A measure whether your music is acoustic. The higher the number the more acoustic sounding your music is.
